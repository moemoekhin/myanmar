Beautiful Bagan

Bagan is an ancient city in central Myanmar(formerly Burma),southwest of Mandalay.Standing on the eastern banks of the Ayeyarwady River,it's known for the Bagan Archaeological Area,where more than 2000 Buddhist monuments tower over green plains.Holy sites around Old Bagan include ornate Anada Tem.ple,built in 1091 and topped with a golden stupa.Nearly is the vast 12th-century Dhammayangyi Temple.

Mingun Pahtodawgyi

The Mingun Pahtodawgyi is an incomplete monument stupa in Mingun,approximately 10 Kilometres northwest of Mandalay.The ruins are the remains of a massive construction project begun by King Bodawpaya in 1970 which was intentionally left unfinished.The pahtodawgyi is seen as the physical manifestations of the well known eccentricties of Bodawpaya.He set up an observation post on an island off Migun to personally supervise the construction of temple.

Hpaung Daw U Pagoda

Hpaung Daw U Pagoda is a notable Buddhist site in Myanmar,located on the Inle lake in Shan State.Surrounding the pagoda,and in the basement are shops selling traditional Shan and Burmese merchandise.It is the most highly revered monastery in the Inle Lake area.The images are of differing sizes,range from about nine to eighteen inches tall.It is believed that the Buddha images were brought to Inlay lake by King Alaungsithu.
